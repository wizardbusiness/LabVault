##### Definition

**`Ansible Core`**

**The open-source automation engine at the heart of the Ansible automation platform — it interprets Ansible *playbooks* (YAML files defining desired system states), connects to Ansible *managed nodes* via SSH without requiring agents, and executes Ansible *modules* to configure servers, deploy applications, and orchestrate infrastructure in a repeatable, *[[Idempotent#Definition|idempotent]]* way.**
