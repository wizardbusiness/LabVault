##### Definition

**`Vector`**

*A mathematical object with both magnitude (size) and direction, represented as an ordered list of numbers that the system uses to represent quantities in space, enable calculations, or encode data.*
##### ---

> [!detail]- More on `vectors`
> 
> Vectors are fundamental throughout computing: in graphics they describe positions and movements, in machine learning they represent data points or features, in physics engines they model forces and velocities, and in databases they enable similarity searches through embeddings. A vector might be written as `[3, 4, 2]` in 3D space or `[0.2, 0.8, 0.1, ...]` as a probability distribution or neural network embedding.
